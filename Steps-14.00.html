Main KPI Cards 14

The Main KPI's are Total Customer, Churn, Churn Rate and  Churn Revenue %


1. Total Customer: For KPI first select Card from Visualizations, drag and drop the count of customer ID in Fields. Click on Format Visual, select call out value font as Arial , Size =25, Bold and color #1F36DF, display unit None and turn off the category Tab.
Click on General, in Properties:
Height = 147, Width =136, Horizontal = 8, Vertical = 73
Title =Total Customer, Arial, 12 Bold, color #1F36DF, Alignment= Middle.
Effect = Background white, Transparency 100%, Visual Border ON, Color =#E6E6E6, Corners 1 px

Measures and Data Modeling 11


1.  Total Customer:   Total Customer = COUNT (telecom_customer_churn[Customer ID])
2.  Churn Customer:  Churn Customer = CALCULATE (COUNT ('telecom_Customer_churn'[Customer Status]), FILTER ('telecom_Customer_churn','telecom_Customer_churn'[Customer Status] = "Churned"))
3.  Total Churn:   Churned_Total = CALCULATE (SUM ('telecom_customer_churn'[Total Revenue]), FILTER ('telecom_Customer_churn','telecom_Customer_churn'[Customer Status] = "Churned"))
4.  Churn Rate:   ChurnRate = 'Measure'[Churn Customer]/[TotalCustomer]
5.  ChurnRateBlank:   ChurnRateBlank = IF (ISBLANK ('Measure'[Churn Customer]/[TotalCustomer]),0,'Measure'[Churn Customer]/[TotalCustomer])
6.  ChurnRevenue%:   ChurnRevenue% = 'Measure'[Churned_Total]/ (Sum ('telecom_customer_churn'[Total Revenue]))
7.  ChurnRevenue%Blk:   ChurnRevenue%Blk = IF(ISBLANK('Measure'[Churned_Total]/Sum ('telecom_customer_churn'[Total Revenue])),0,'Measure'[Churned_Total]/ Sum ('telecom_customer_churn'[Total Revenue]))

Transforming the Data in Power Query Editor 10
Let’s clean the data in Power Query Editor

1.    First remove Latitude and Longitude from the data.
2.    In the report there are lot of blank columns in Multiple Line, Internet type, Online             Security, Online Backup, Device Protection Plan, Premium Tech Support, Streaming         TV, Streaming Movies, Unlimited Data replace it all with “Null”.





3.    Next, I create a duplicate copy for customer status to replace Join with Stayed to get the total cx who are with the company (Stayed + Joined) and the total cx who are churned (Churned).



4.  Let’s add Conditional Column to find out the VIP customer on the basis of Monthly  charge  = Table.AddColumn (#"Replaced Value12", "VIP Customer", each if [Monthly Charge] >=75 then "Yes" else if [Monthly Charge] <= 75 then "No" else null)


5. Another conditional column flor adding the tenure.
    = Table.AddColumn(#"Replaced Value30", "Tenure Year", each if [Tenure in Months]  >= 24 then "2 Year and Higher" else if [Tenure in Months] >= 12 then "1 Year to 2 Year" else if [Tenure in Months] <= 12 then "Less than 1 year" else null)



6.  This is where we are done with cleaning transforming the data Click on close & Apply. Get the other table telecom_customer_churn too by clicking on the get data.
